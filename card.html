Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone lethal sting.

  • Scientific name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average lifespan: 7 years
  • Average length: 11 centimeters
  • Habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemone fish are among the most recognizable among all reef dwellers. They reach about 11 centimeters in length, and are named multicoloured sea anemone in which they make their homes.
